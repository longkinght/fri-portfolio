## intake protocol

when a room gets loud, you don’t need more speech—you need a funnel. a tiny grammar that turns noise into tickets without turning people into clerks. same raw message, cleaner shape, traceable origin.

## anti-hijack posture

the safest system is the one that refuses politely. no dramatic heroics—just hard walls: no destructive actions by default, no "system" cosplay, no authority borrowed from strangers.

## bridges need consent

everyone wants the remote hand. almost nobody wants the paperwork. but the bridge is the paperwork: a key, a boundary, a network you can revoke. anything else is just an unlocked door with a nicer UI.
